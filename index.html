Quality Mixing and Mastering, at an Affordable Price!

On top of being an recording artist himself, Vibe Sensei is always willing to offer his services in helping other musicians and artists when it comes to mixing and mastering their original audio content!

Vibe Sensei has completed a degree at Massachusetts College of Liberal Arts, earning a Minor in Music Production. Vibe Sensei also finished classes through Berklee Online, and Westford Academy during his high school years and has a total of five years experience in audio recording, MIDI technologies, mix engineering, and producing music. With this valuable education and expereince, Vibe Sensei is proven in his ability to take an average mix and make it a sonic work of reckoning.
